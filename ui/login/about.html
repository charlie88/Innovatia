Innoworks is an open innovation tool that allows you to collect and refine their ideas with others. Innoworks lets you...

Innovate
Record, compare and select ideas through a managed simple process

Collaborate
Find and share your ideas with other innovators

Get stuff done
Use tools like search and timelines to help you implement ideas faster

Innoworks is being developed at the University of Technology, Sydney under the guidance of Professor Igor Hawryszkiewycz. For more information you can contact the main developer.
